My passion has been Computer Engineering for as long as I can remember. I enjoy the software as well as the hardware aspects of Computer Engineering. My work has usually reflected that.

My first work experience came with MAYU (now called Type Art). This is an iOS app with which you can create beautiful animated texts. I worked with a startup in Israel developing this app when I was in High School, using my iOS app development knowledge.

The summer of freshman year of college, I interned at a company called BioEye. I worked on a project involving machine learning to provide a mobile eye tracking solution. I also worked on their back end, using technologies like Node.js and MongoDB.

In Summer 2019, I worked as a Software Engineering intern at National Instruments. This was my first experience working at a larger Computer Engineering company. I worked on a project involving embedded development through which I learned a lot about how hardware and software works together.

I have also worked as a Computer Science Peer Teacher and Physics Undergraduate Teaching Fellow at Texas A&M University, regarding non Computer Science jobs. I will be interning at Boeing next summer in Seattle.
