About Me

My name is Anuj Parakh. I am a Junior Computer Engineering (Computer Science track) student at Texas A&M University.

I am originally from Rajasthan, India. I was born in Seattle, Washington and I moved to Rajasthan, India when I was 4 years old. I moved again to Gujarat, India when I turned 8. Finally, I moved to Austin, Texas when I turned 18 for college.

I got my first contact with Computer Engineering when I was pretty young. My father, a Computer Engineer himself, first introduced me to programming. At the same time, I was getting interested in Electronics. Through the combination of the two, I started working on simple projects involving iOS, Android, MacOS, Arduinos, and simple circuits.

I love travelling, reading, watching movies and TV shows, coffee, music, and playing sports. A hobby of mine is to start on random personal projects, a lot of which don't always work out. If I'm not working on schoolwork or a project, I will most likely be immersed in a new book or watching some TV show.
