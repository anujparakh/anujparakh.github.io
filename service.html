Service

Visamo Kids Foundation

Visamo Kids Foundation is a non-profit organization in India that takes in kids who cannot afford a good education. I volunteered with them to tutor a few children with basic Math and English concepts.

The Big Event

The Big Event is the largest one-day, student-run service project in the nation. We do simple tasks like yard work, window washing and painting for residents of the community in Bryan/College Station.

Texas A&M Physics Festival

The Texas A&M Physics Festival is a Physics Festival held by the University every year. I volunteer in this festival to help set up and demonstrate some of these experiments.

Aggie School Volunteers

Aggie School Volunteers is a student run organization at Texas A&M University with the goal of enhancing the lives of children by having members act as a weekly tutor, mentor, or lunch buddy in local public schools.
