Service

Visamo Kids Foundation

Visamo Kids Foundation is a non-profit organization in India that takes in kids who cannot afford a good education. They have created a facility that can host more than a 100 children with mission to transform the lives of less privileged yet high potential kids through formal K-12 education in English. I volunteered with them to tutor a few children with basic Math and English concepts.

Texas A&M Physics Festival

The Texas A&M Physics Festival is a Physics Festival held by the University every year. They have over 200 amazing, interactive physics demonstrations, specialized programs by age groups, and public lectures by prominent scientists and astronauts. I volunteer in this festival to help set up and demonstrate some of these experiments.

The Big Event

Since its introduction in 1982, The Big Event has become the largest one-day, student-run service project in the nation. Each spring, tens of thousands of Texas A&M students come together to say "Thank You" to the residents of Bryan and College Station. We do simple tasks like yard work, window washing and painting for residents of the community.
Click on the title to learn more about The Big Event.

Aggie School Volunteers

Aggie School Volunteer is a student run organization at Texas A&M University with the goal of enhancing the lives of children by having members act as a weekly tutor, mentor, or lunch buddy in local public schools. I was a part of this organization and went to middle schools and elementary schools to tutor and help children.
